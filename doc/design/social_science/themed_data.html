Themed Data

ADA Social Science currently hold over 2000 data sets covering topics from across the social sciences. ADA, in conjunction with leading Australian researchers, have established a series of thematic guides to content in the ADA Social Science holdings, for detailed information on specialized materials within the archive.

Thematic guides are available for the following topics:

  • The Australian Survey of Social Attitudes
  • The National Social Science Survey
  • The Australian Election Studies
  • Graduate Destination Surveys
  • Australian public opinion polls

If you have additional ideas for thematic content from the ADA Social Science holdings, please contact ADA at info@ada.edu.au.
